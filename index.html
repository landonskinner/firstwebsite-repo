about me

My final project for the pre-work section is using my new skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to design my very own website.

My goal for creating this site is to introduce myself both inside and outside of a software engineering setting. I hope for you to learn more about who I am, and I aim to document my progress throughout my bootcamp experience.
